Frequent Questions

What is Echo?

Echo is an advanced AI assistant designed to help with daily tasks, answer queries, and provide entertainment through voice and text.

How do I use voice commands?

Simply tap the microphone button or the floating mic icon on mobile, then speak naturally. Ensure your microphone permissions are enabled.

Is it free?

Yes, Echo is completely open-source and free to use for personal projects.
